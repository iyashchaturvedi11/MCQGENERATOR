## MCQ 1
Question: What was unusual about the fruits of the tree in Eldermere?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They grew in clusters like grapes.
C) They shimmered with a golden hue.
D) They tasted like chocolate.
Correct Answer: C) They shimmered with a golden hue.

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe would happen to anyone who tasted a Shakespear?
A) They would become invisible.
B) They would gain a glimpse into their future.
C) They would be granted eternal youth.
D) They would be able to speak to animals.
Correct Answer: B) They would gain a glimpse into their future.

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream before she tasted the Shakespear?
A) To become a painter.
B) To become a musician.
C) To become a writer.
D) To become a dancer.
Correct Answer: C) To become a writer.

## MCQ 4
Question: After tasting the Shakespear, what did Emma see in her visions?
A) A dark and desolate wasteland.
B) Herself standing on a grand stage and wandering through enchanted forests.
C) A hidden treasure buried beneath the tree.
D) A prophecy of the village's destruction.
Correct Answer: B) Herself standing on a grand stage and wandering through enchanted forests.

## MCQ 5
Question: What happened after Emma published her book?
A) The Shakespear tree withered and died.
B) The villagers forgot about the tree and moved away.
C) The book captured the hearts of many beyond Eldermere.
D) A rival village declared war on Eldermere.
Correct Answer: C) The book captured the hearts of many beyond Eldermere.
