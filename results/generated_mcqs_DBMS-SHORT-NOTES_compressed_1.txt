## MCQ
Question: Which of the following is NOT a limitation of File Systems compared to DBMS?
A) No Data Abstraction
B) Not Scalable
C) No Concurrency Control
D) Built-in transaction management
Correct Answer: D) Built-in transaction management

## MCQ
Question: According to Armstrong's Axioms, which rule states: "If X→Y, then XZ→YZ"?
A) Reflexivity
B) Augmentation
C) Transitivity
D) Decomposition
Correct Answer: B) Augmentation

## MCQ
Question: What is the term for a minimal superkey in a relational database?
A) Super-key
B) Alternate Key
C) Candidate Key
D) Primary Key
Correct Answer: C) Candidate Key

## MCQ
Question: Which normal form eliminates transitive dependencies?
A) First Normal Form (1NF)
B) Second Normal Form (2NF)
C) Third Normal Form (3NF)
D) Boyce-Codd Normal Form (BCNF)
Correct Answer: C) Third Normal Form (3NF)

## MCQ
Question: In ER modeling, what does a double line connecting an entity to a relationship indicate?
A) Partial participation
B) Total participation
C) Weak entity
D) Derived attribute
Correct Answer: B) Total participation
