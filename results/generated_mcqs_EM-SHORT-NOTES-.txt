## MCQ
Question: What is the condition for two matrices A and B to be equal?
A) They must have the same number of rows.
B) They must have the same number of columns.
C) They must have the same order and corresponding elements must be equal.
D) They must be square matrices.
Correct Answer: C) They must have the same order and corresponding elements must be equal.

A) The area of a parallelogram.

Question: According to the text, what is the necessary and sufficient condition for a homogeneous system of linear equations (AA = 0) to have non-trivial solutions?
A) The number of equations must be equal to the number of variables.
B) The determinant of A must be non-zero.
